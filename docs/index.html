Bio

I am currently a fifth-year Ph.D student in Computer Science at the University of Chicago. I am a member of UChicago's SUPERGroup, where I am advised by Dr. Blase Ur. My research involves working with Large Language Models (LLMs) in a privacy and security context. I am interested in using LLMs as tools to preserve end-user privacy and security, as well as developing principled approaches for making LLMs safer tools to use in other contexts.


I am also proudly Texan (born n' raised), biracial, bisexual, and nonbinary (they/them). I powerlift, play music, and enjoy very nerdy tabletop games in my free time.
